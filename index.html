FAQ

got a question?

Why do I need this?

If you received an email or in-app message directing you to this page, it is because your account is currently listening more than 35 hours per month. The tier you are currently using was created for personal use with under 35 hours of listening time per month. Since we pay music licensing on a per play basis, we have to begin enforcing the 35 hour a month limit, as we actually lose money on accounts that play over 35 hours a month.

I’m ready to Upgrade to FITRADIO for Gyms and Studios! How do I cancel my current subscription?

Because FITRADIO uses a number of payment options (App Store, Google Play, Amazon, and Stripe), you will need to cancel your current subscription when upgrading to the FITRADIO for Gyms and Studios Tier if you do not want to be charged for both memberships.
iPhone
  1. Open the settings app on your iPhone
  2. Tap your name
  3. Tap Subscriptions
  4. Tap on your FITRADIO subscription
  5. Tap Cancel Subscription
Android
  1. Open the Google Play app
  2. At the top right, tap the profile icon
  3. Tap payments & subsctipsions (choose subscriptions)
  4. Select the FITRADIO subscription
  5. Tap cancel subscription
Paid through website
  1. Go to Profile Settings in the FITRADIO app
  2. Select "Edit Profile"
  3. Select "Account Info"
  4. Select "Manage Account"
  5. Select "Cancel Plan"
Paid through website (Amazon)
  1. Go to Your Memberships and Subscriptions on Amazon
  2. Select Manage Subscription next to the FITRADIO subscription
  3. Select the link under Advance Controls that the subscription page opens. From there, you can cancel the subscription.

What happens if I don’t switch to the Business Tier? 

You will still be able to listen to FITRADIO for 35 hours a month. After 35 hours, the app will let you know you have reached your limit for the month and will restrict access.
